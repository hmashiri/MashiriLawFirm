PERSONAL INJURY

Have you been injured in a car accident, had a slip or trip and fall, or been attacked by a dog?

We handle numerous types of injuries, including injuries arising out of Motor Vehicle Accidents, Slip or Trip & Falls, and Dog Bites. Whether YOUR injury is large or small, YOU should contact us immediately to learn about your legal rights. What may seem like a small injury today, can become much more serious months or years from now.

We represent injured victims throughout California for physical injuries, emotional pain and financial damages, including but not limited to:

  • Medical Bills
  • Pain and Suffering
  • Physical Disability
  • Disfigurement
  • Permanent Scars
  • Emotional Trauma
  • Mental Anguish
  • Property Damage
  • Loss Consortium
  • Lost Wages

    Motor Vehicle Accidents:

    If you have been injured in a motor vehicle accident, you may be able to file an accident claim in order to recover necessary compensation. We represent individuals in a wide variety of motor vehicle cases, including:

  • Car accidents
  • Truck accidents
  • Motorcycle accidents
  • Bicycle accidents
  • Bus accidents
  • Other motor vehicle accidents

    Slip or Trip and Fall:

    If you slip, fall, trip or are otherwise injured in a store, you may have a valid legal claim against that business and may be entitled to recover necessary compensation. We represent individuals in a wide variety of premises liability cases, including:

  • Slip and Fall
  • Trip and Fall
  • Falling Objects
  • Accidents at private residences
  • Bar fights
  • Assault injuries
  • Insufficient security

    Dog Bite:

    If you have been attacked and bitten by a dog, you may be entitled to recover necessary compensation. Although dogs are called a “man’s best friend”, some dogs are vicious, dangerous, and bite, causing physical injuries. According to Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, there are more than 4.7 million people who suffer from dog bite injuries. If you have been the victim of a dog bite injury, contact us for the help you deserve.
