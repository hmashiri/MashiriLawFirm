Wrongful Termination:

Wrongful termination occurs whenever an employee is fired illegally. California is an at-will employment state, permitting employers to terminate employees for any reason. This does not mean, however, that employees do not have rights. An employer may not fire you in violation of California or federal laws.If you were fired or forced to resign from your job, you may be able to take action against your former employer. We can review your claim and determine if your employer violated your rights.
